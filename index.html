Our Story

Founded in 2010, Slice Heaven began as a small family-owned pizzeria in the heart of the city. Our founder, Chef Marco, brought authentic Italian pizza-making techniques from Naples to create the perfect pizza experience.

Today, we continue that tradition with the same passion and dedication to quality ingredients and craftsmanship that made us famous.
